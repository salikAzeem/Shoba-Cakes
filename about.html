HISTORY OF SHOBA CAKES

SHOBA CAKES is a place where the fragrant aroma of fresh bread and the warmth of family meet each other.

Standing majestically on a bustling street corner, this bakery has a long story that began with passion and a love for the art of cooking.

With the family's savings money and his mother's inherited recipe, she opened SHOBA CAKES in a small room on the outskirts of VIZAG.

Shoba is famous for her creativity in creating various types of bread with a unique touch.

It combines traditional recipes with fresh ingredients and modern innovations, creating flavors that keep customers coming back.
